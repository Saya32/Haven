Our Promise

At Haven, we believe everyone deserves the opportunity to rebuild their lives. Our mission is to support Category C homeless individuals by connecting them with potential employers, providing resources, and fostering a community of support.

  • Below is how we will achieve this:
  • Focus group with partners: with our partners we hope to validate our problem and our platform to get a deep understanding, and improve!
  • Pilate with Homewards in Lambeth: we want to validate our product with larger numbers in collaboration with Prince Williams.
  • Establish Haven as a national policy: with Homeless Link's policy team and Rough Sleeping Team of Mayor of London, we will push Haven as a national policy.

How we started? Who we are?

Founded in 2024, Haven emerged from the realization that many individuals experiencing homelessness possess the skills and potential to thrive but lack the connections to secure suitable employment.

We are Haven, a student-led social enterprise from King‚Äôs College London. Our mission is to support homeless individuals by helping them reintegrate into society. Our efforts specifically address Category C homelessness, which includes individuals whose housing needs meet council standards but do not urgently require rehousing. This group makes up 50% of applicants.

Our solution is a website that connects Category C homeless individuals with potential employers. We aim to collaborate with existing platforms and concepts, leveraging ideas, strategies, and connections to scale this initiative nationally through strong employer partnerships.
